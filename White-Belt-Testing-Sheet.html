The Three Basic Rules of Martial Arts
1. Don't use it the wrong way!
Never abuse your Martial Arts skills.
2. Be respectful.
Show respect to your instructors, fellow students, and everyone around you.
3. Do your best.
Always give your maximum effort in everything you do.
The Three Basic Stances
1. Attention
Feet together, hands at your sides, standing straight and still.
2. Chun Bi (Ready Stance)
Feet apart, hands in fists at belt level.
3. Fighting Stance
Right foot steps back, hands come up in a guard position.
