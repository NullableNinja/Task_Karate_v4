Rules of TASK Karate

The foundations of our dojo. Click to learn more.

1
Don't use it the wrong way!

This is Rule #1 for a reason. Martial arts gives you power‚Äîthe power to defend yourself and others. But with great power comes great responsibility!

  • Never use karate to bully or intimidate
  • Only use skills for self-defense as a last resort
  • Protect those who cannot protect themselves
  • Walk away from fights‚Äîreal strength is self-control
"The ultimate aim of karate lies not in victory or defeat, but in the perfection of character." ‚Äî Gichin Funakoshi
2
Be respectful!

Respect is the foundation of martial arts. We bow when entering and leaving the dojo. We address instructors properly. We treat our training partners with care.

  • Listen when others are speaking
  • Follow instructions the first time
  • Take care of your uniform and equipment
  • Encourage your fellow students
  • Respect yourself‚Äîyou're worth the effort!
"Respect is earned, honor is given, and loyalty is demonstrated."
3
Do your best!

Your best is always good enough. We don't compare ourselves to others‚Äîonly to who we were yesterday. Progress over perfection!

  • Give 100% effort in every class
  • Practice at home (even just 5 minutes helps!)
  • Ask questions when you don't understand
  • Get back up when you fall
  • Celebrate small victories
"A black belt is just a white belt who never gave up."
