Programs

Something for every age and energy level

Choose a program to learn more.

Kids Karate

Ages 5–12. Classes blend fun drills with serious life skills—focus, listening, confidence, responsibility, and respect. Kids learn discipline through positive structure, develop coordination, and earn stripes and belts the traditional way: with effort, attitude, and consistency.

Teens & Adults

A practical blend of striking, self-defense, pad work, conditioning, and controlled partner drills. Teens and adults get a full-body workout, build confidence, reduce stress, and develop real-world skills without the ego or intensity of a full-contact school.

IS3 • Eskrima

Inayan System III (IS3) is a Filipino martial art rooted in the seven styles passed down from Mangisursuro Mike Inay and refined by Suro Emanuel Hart. Students learn timing, footwork, weapon handling, flow, and transitions between long, mid, and close range.

Weapons

Structured practice with bo staff, nunchaku, escrima sticks, and traditional training tools. Students build coordination, control, and distance awareness using safe padded or wooden trainers before progressing to more advanced techniques.
